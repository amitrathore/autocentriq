FAQ

Questions operators usually ask first.

What is AutocentrIQ?

AutocentrIQ is an automotive Outcome Intelligence platform that helps dealerships and service businesses identify, prioritize, execute, and measure the actions that improve revenue, margin, productivity, retention, and cash flow.

Is AutocentrIQ a DMS or CRM?

No. AutocentrIQ works above the systems of record and engagement already used by the business.

Does AutocentrIQ replace our current systems?

No. It is designed to make existing systems more valuable by turning their data into prioritized, measurable action.

What parts of the business can it support?

AutocentrIQ can support sales, service, inventory, marketing, customer retention, and operating management.

Does it only provide recommendations?

No. Depending on the configuration, AutocentrIQ can recommend actions, prepare workflows for approval, or execute approved action types automatically.

How is customer data protected?

AutocentrIQ should support role-based access, secure data handling, configurable permissions, audit logs, and action-level governance. Final website claims must match implemented security capabilities.
